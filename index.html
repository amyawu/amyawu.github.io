Latest pitstop: Berkeley, CA

I build AI for accelerated scientific discovery — autonomous systems for experimental design and scientific workflows at national user facilities.

Right now I’m a research intern at the Advanced Light Source at Berkeley Lab, where I build 3D magnetic models and digital twins for the ALS-U upgrade and evaluate large language models and agentic AI for accelerator operations.

I earned my B.S. with Honors in Computer Science (minor in Digital Arts & Sciences) from the University of Florida in 2025, advised by Dr. Christan Grant (UF Data Studio) and Dr. Jeremy A. Magruder Waisome (PRISEM Lab). Along the way I’ve researched across labs and universities — Argonne, Columbia / Amazon, RWTH Aachen, and Dartmouth — spanning large language models, computer vision, materials ML, and mixed-methods CS-education research.

I’m now applying to PhD programs in AI. If any of this resonates, I’d love to hear from you.
